## üì± Photo Tips for Non-Professionals

You don't need a professional photographer. Modern phones work great!

### Lighting
- **Best time:** Overcast days or early morning (no harsh shadows)
- **Interior:** Turn on all lights, open blinds
- **Avoid:** Noon sun, backlit subjects

### Composition
- **Rule of thirds:** Don't center everything
- **Include people when possible:** Even partially in frame
- **Multiple angles:** Take 5-10 versions of each shot

### What to Capture
- **Candid > Posed:** Natural moments beat lineup photos
- **Action shots:** People doing things, not looking at camera
- **Details + Wide:** Get both close-ups and full scenes

### Technical
- **Clean the lens:** Seriously, wipe your phone camera
- **Horizontal for web:** Most web images work better landscape
- **High resolution:** Don't crop in-camera, crop later
