## Quick Brand Overview

### The Brand in One Sentence
Gulf South Homes is a **family-owned, trustworthy, modern** home dealer that makes quality affordable homes accessible to Southeast Louisiana families—without the corporate coldness or cheap vibes.

### Brand Personality
- ✅ **Trustworthy & Transparent** — 30 years, BBB accredited, no hidden fees
- ✅ **Modern & Professional** — Contemporary design, premium execution
- ✅ **Warm & Community-Focused** — Louisiana-rooted, family values
- ✅ **Accessible Yet Premium** — Quality homes, not luxury prices
- ✅ **Problem-Solvers** — One-stop shop from selection to delivery

### What We're NOT
- ❌ Cheap/discount (no garish banners, no "bargain basement" vibes)
- ❌ Corporate/cold (we're family-owned and human)
- ❌ Trendy/temporary (our homes last 30+ years)
- ❌ Sketchy (we're transparent and honest)

## Logo System

### Primary Logo

**Logo Mark:**
- Modern, geometric design
- Incorporates Gulf (water/waves) and Home (roof/structure)
- Color: Gulf Navy Blue (#1E3A5F)
- Style: Clean, professional, instantly recognizable

**Logo Variations:**
1. **Full Logo** (Logo + "Gulf South Homes" text)
   - Use: Main branding, website header, large formats
   - Minimum size: 200px width

2. **Logo Mark Only** (Icon without text)
   - Use: Favicon, small spaces, social icons
   - Minimum size: 32px width

3. **Horizontal Lock-up** (Logo + text side-by-side)
   - Use: Business cards, email signatures
   - Minimum size: 150px width

4. **Vertical Stack** (Logo above text)
   - Use: Instagram profiles, certificates, posters
   - Minimum size: 100px width

5. **White Logo** (Reverse on dark backgrounds)
   - Use: Dark hero sections, navy backgrounds
   - Color: White (#FFFFFF)

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Don't change the colors
- ❌ Don't add effects (drop shadows, glows, outlines)
- ❌ Don't place on busy/complex backgrounds
- ❌ Don't use below minimum size (32px)
- ❌ Don't rotate at awkward angles

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space around logo: 10% of logo height
- No text or images should touch the logo boundary

## Brand Imagery & Photography

### Photography Style

**What We Shoot:**
- ✅ Real families in/around homes (authentic, warm)
- ✅ Home interiors (bright, clean, professional lighting)
- ✅ Exterior homes (daytime, well-maintained, beautiful)
- ✅ Team members (approachable, professional attire)
- ✅ Happy customers (genuine smiles, testimonial style)

**What We Avoid:**
- ❌ Stock photo vibes (too generic, artificial)
- ❌ Dark/gloomy interiors
- ❌ Dated furniture or appliances
- ❌ Overly staged/artificial setups
- ❌ Sad or forced expressions

### Color Tone in Photos
- Warm, natural lighting (golden hour when possible)
- Slightly saturated (not washed out, not oversaturated)
- Consistent color grading across all images
- Louisiana landscape aesthetic (greenery, water, open spaces)

### Photography Grid
- Aim for variety: Mix wide shots and close-ups
- Show progression: Outside → Living room → Kitchen → Bedrooms
- Include lifestyle: Families using the space, not just empty rooms
- Quality over quantity: 5 stunning photos beat 20 mediocre ones
