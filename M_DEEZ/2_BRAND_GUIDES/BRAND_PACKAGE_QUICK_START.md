## Logo Quick Rules

✅ Use these sizes: 200px (full), 100px (vertical), 32px (icon)
✅ Keep white space around (10% of logo height)
✅ Use Navy blue (#1E3A5F) as primary color
✅ White (#FFFFFF) on dark backgrounds

❌ Don't stretch, distort, or change colors
❌ Don't use below 32px width
❌ Don't add shadows or effects
❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds

## Social Media Quick Guide

**Colors to use:**
- Post backgrounds: Navy (#1E3A5F) or Light Gray (#F0F0F0)
- Headlines: Outfit font, navy or white
- CTAs: Red button or blue link
- Images: High-quality homes or happy families

**Caption tone:**
- Warm and welcoming
- Action-oriented ("Schedule your tour now!")
- Include phone number: (985) 876-0222
- Ask questions ("Which home style fits your family?")

**Post types:**
1. Featured home showcase (image + details + CTA)
2. Customer testimonial (quote + photo + location)
3. Financing tips ("How to get approved in 24 hours")
4. Team introduction (photo + bio + warmth)
5. "Did you know?" facts (Louisiana homes, manufacturing)

## Design Checklist

Before sending any deliverable:

**Color Check:**
- [ ] Navy (#1E3A5F) used correctly for headings
- [ ] Red (#D32F2F) used ONLY for primary CTAs
- [ ] Blue (#4A90E2) used for secondary actions
- [ ] Proper contrast (text readable on backgrounds)

**Typography Check:**
- [ ] Headlines: Outfit 600 or 700
- [ ] Body: Plus Jakarta 400
- [ ] Buttons: Plus Jakarta 600 uppercase
- [ ] Size hierarchy clear (H1 > H2 > body)

**Logo Check:**
- [ ] Logo at least 32px (or 1" in print)
- [ ] Clear space respected (10% around logo)
- [ ] Correct color (Navy or white)
- [ ] No distortion or effects

**Messaging Check:**
- [ ] Warm and professional tone
- [ ] Clear CTA (what's the next step?)
- [ ] Includes contact info or CTA
- [ ] No hype or false claims

**Image Check:**
- [ ] High quality (not pixelated)
- [ ] Authentic (real families, not stock photos)
- [ ] On-brand (warm lighting, professional)
- [ ] Properly licensed or owned

## Quick Decision Tree

**"What color?"**
- Heading → Navy (#1E3A5F)
- Action button → Red (#D32F2F)
- Secondary button → Blue (#4A90E2)
- Background → Light Gray (#F0F0F0) or Navy
- Text on dark → White (#FFFFFF)

**"What font?"**
- Headline → Outfit (bold/semibold)
- Body → Plus Jakarta (regular/medium)
- Button → Plus Jakarta semibold, uppercase

**"What to say?"**
- Primary CTA → "Schedule Tour" or "Get Quote"
- Secondary → "Learn More" or "View Options"
- General → Use one of the 5 key messages

**"Is it on-brand?"**
- Does it sound warm and expert? ✅ Good
- Does it have clear next steps? ✅ Good
- Is it honest (no exaggeration)? ✅ Good
- Would a family feel welcome? ✅ Good
