DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

Color Psychology Journey:
  Week 1:    primary to blue-600      (Trust & beginning)
  Week 2-3:  blue-600 to blue-500     (Preparation)
  Week 4-6:  blue-500 to accent       (Action & delivery)
  Week 7-8:  accent to red-600        (Completion energy)
  Week 8+:   red-600 to red-700       (Celebration!)

Interactive Elements:
  - Week badges scale 110% on hover

  - Border colors transition (stone-200 to primary/30)
  - Task titles change color (stone-900 to primary)
  - Subtle gradient overlay on card hover

  Desktop (1920px):  Full timeline spine, 2-column layout
